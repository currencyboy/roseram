Yes! Most exchanges support withdrawing to all these addresses.

### How long until my balance updates?
- Solana: ~10 seconds (automatic)
- Bitcoin: 10-60 minutes (blockchain dependent)
- Ethereum: 15-30 seconds (blockchain dependent)
- BNB: 5-10 seconds (blockchain dependent)

### Is my payment safe?
âœ… Yes! We only receive payments. No risk to you.

### Can I get a refund?
Bitcoin and Ethereum transfers are **permanent**. Check the address before sending!

### What if I send wrong amount?
We'll add whatever you send. No partial refunds.

### Can I use a different wallet?
Yes! You can send from any wallet or exchange that supports that blockchain.

### Minimum amounts?
- Solana: 0.01 SOL (~$2)
- Bitcoin: 0.0001 BTC (~$4)
- Ethereum: 0.001 ETH (~$2)
- BNB: 0.01 BNB (~$3)

### I sent payment but no confirmation?
1. Wait 5-10 minutes (blockchain may be slow)
2. Check balance manually (refresh page)
3. Search address on blockchain explorer
4. Verify amount was correct

### My exchange won't send to this address
Some exchanges block certain addresses. Try:
1. Different exchange
2. Different wallet app
3. Contact exchange support
